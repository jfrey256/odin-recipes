Cheese & Crackers

Description

Ahh, some crackers. Also, some cheese! You can put a piece of cheese on a single cracker, or, make a cheese and cracker sandwhich! Awesome!

Ingredients

  • Cheese, any kind except american probably.
  • Crackers. Ritz, saltines, etc. You know the drill.
  • A good attitude!

Steps

  1. Cut up that damn cheese (unless it is already sliced)!
  2. Put that cheese on a plate all nice like.
  3. Pop open those crackers.
  4. Put them crackers on a plate next to the cheese.
  5. Now it's time to DIG IN!
