Nachos

Description

Tortilla chips with warm nacho cheese sauce. An American Classic. Where does the cheese come from? Nobody knows, but is is good!

So how do you make this delicious dish? Here's what you need:

Ingredients

  • Any old tortilla chips from the local grocer.
  • Buy some cheese sauce, or make your own, I don't care.
  • A microwave, fire, heat source, something to heat up that cheese.

Steps

  1. Tear open that bag of tortilla chips, maybe put them in a bowl if you have one.
  2. Crack open that thing of cheese.
  3. Put some cheese in a microwavable bowl or whatever.
  4. Microwave that cheese for 45 seconds or something.
  5. DIG IN! Don't stop eating til you have a stomach ache.
