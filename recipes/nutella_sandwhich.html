Nutella Sandwhich

Description

Delicious hazelnutty nutella sandwhich on wheat bread.

Ingredients

  • Wheat bread
  • Nutella

Steps

  1. Break out that wheat bread, grab a couple slices.
  2. Pop open that nutella, grab a butter knife.
  3. Spread that hazelnut spread all up on that wheat bread.
  4. Close that sandwhich and DIG IN!
